Use basic sentences  

Where is Dad?                -      Kde je táta?    
Dad is at home.              -      Táta je doma.  
Jitka has a bicycle.         -      Jitka má kolo.   
Is Mom at home?              -      Je máma doma?   
She is here.                 -      One je tady.   
Jakub is eating a banana.    -      Jakub jí banán.
