Japanese has three writing systems.  

In **hiragana**, which you can use to write most Japanese words, each character represents a syllable, and there are no spaces between words.  

KEY PHRASES  

Order food  

すし、ください。  
Sushi, please.  

おちゃ、ください！  
Green tea, please!  

ごはんとみず、ください！  
Rice and water, please!  

みずです。  
It's water.   

We use **romanji** to represent Japanese sounds using the Roman alphabet.   

すしとみず  
[sushi to mizu]  
sushi and water  

In Japanese, verbs go at the end:  
すしです。  

[sushi desu]  
It's sushi.
