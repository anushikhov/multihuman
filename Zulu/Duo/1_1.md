## Use basic greetings  
  
Sawubona!  
Hello!  
  
Unjani, baba?  
How are you, dad?  
  
Ngiyaphila.  
I am well.  
  
Sala kahle, nkosazana.  
Stay well, ma'am.  
  
Hamba kahle, mkhulu.  
Go well, grandpa.
