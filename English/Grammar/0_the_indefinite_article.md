### a/an (the indefinite article)  

The form **a** is used before words beginning with a consonant, or a vowel with a consonant sound: _a man, a hat, a university, a European, a one-way street_  

The form **an** is used before words beginning with a vowel **(a, e, i, o, u)** or words beginning with a mute **h**:  _an apple, an island, an uncle, an egg, an onion, an hour_ or individual letters spoken with a vowel sound: _an L-plate, an MP, an SOS, an 'x'_.  

**a/an** is the same for all genders: _a man, a woman, an actor, an acress, a table._
